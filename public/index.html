How to maximize your results using LegFlex board?

Let us get you the best out of this product in 3 easy steps

Step 1:

Take your LegFlex board out of the box andplace it on a flat and clean surface. Optionally you can place your board near a wall so that you can balance yourself.

Step 2:

There are 4 levels provided in the board for exercise. You can adjust the board according to your need, with 1st level being easy and 4th being the most challenging.

Step 3:

Now, you can start performing any exercises on your LegFlex board. Perform each set for 30 seconds. Take a break, stretch and shake your legs and repeat the same process for 3 times each day.
